Inertia: The Resistance

Inertia was created originally to be a comic book. However, as time progressed, I realized I wanted to do more than the limitations of a comic. Thus, I began to create Inertia as a visual novel.

In Inertia you can choose between two different characters.

  • Ian
  • Rikku

Inertia was intended for you to choose your own story. Yes, that means not only can you choose your character but you get to see different story lines based on the decisions you choose!

Make sure you play both characters stories because you'll find something new every single playthrough!
