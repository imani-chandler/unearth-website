Unearth The Characters

Mason

"I won't let you get in my way!

The last recruited member of Scarab, Mason is a "Jack-of-All-Trades". There is almost nothing Mason won't do to finish the mission. Mason is numb to the world and those around him. Can he ever feel again?

Emily

"Bring it on!"

The fire-cracker of Scarab, Emily is a master explosive technician. Whether it's buildings or people, there's no wall that she can't break with her personality and skillset. Just make sure to never make get on her bad side!

Judah

"I saw right past you!"

A former military captain turned traitor, Judah joined Scarab to make the changes that he couldn't as a soldier. A leading marksman, Judah sees things before others can even process it. Not to mention, he's also the wisecrack of Scarab.

Jay

"You're going to have to go through me first!"

The founder and learder of Scarab. Jay has always put his comrades or should I say "family" first. He's always the first to adverse danger and will do anything to protect his team. With just his two pistols, you would think you're facing against an army.
