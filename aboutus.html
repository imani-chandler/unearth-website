Unearth The Author

How Unearth Began

Unearth began as a way to tell my story. From watching my grandmother battle cancer, to losing my ex-fiance, Unearth takes inspiration from pivotal moments of my life. Each character incorporates a different aspect of who I am as a person. As I evolve and grow as a person, so does my story.

My Journey

I've always been a writer of sorts. I started writing poetry and songs since I was in elmentary school. I've always wanted to create a comic book, but I wanted to show more of my creative side. When I stumbled onto visual novels, I realized I found what I was looking for! Unearth was the start of me becoming a novelest, and also the start of me learning to code.

What's To Come

DLC! I plan on adding the last mercenary as a playable character after I finish the novel as an expansion to it. After that, I plan on bringing the other stories I have to life as well after Unearth is complete. Prepare for the next big titles in the future, Inertia and Exhilerate, and welcome to my portfolio!
